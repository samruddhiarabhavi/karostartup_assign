About Karo Pitch

Built for
Founders from
Every Corner
of India.

Thousands of founders are building real, impactful businesses — in Jaipur, Coimbatore, Patna, and everywhere in between. Yet most struggle to even get a 30-minute meeting with an investor.

Karo Pitch was created to change that. We connect deserving founders with investors through structured, curated pitch events — no warm introductions required.

🏙️ Tier-2 & Tier-3 Focus 🤝 Curated Matchmaking 📺 Live Pitch Events 🇮🇳 Bharat-First
🎯

The Mission

India's most accessible startup discovery and funding platform — empowering every founder regardless of geography or network.

🏛️

The Format

Closed-room investor pitch events where selected startups present live to a curated investor panel.

📣

The Visibility

Get featured across KaroStartup's media platform — reaching thousands of founders, investors and industry leaders.

The Process

From Idea to Investment
in 4 Simple Steps

A transparent, founder-friendly process designed to give every startup a fair shot at funding.

📋

Apply with Your Pitch Deck

Submit your application with a brief about your startup, the problem you're solving, and your ask.

🔍

Get Shortlisted by KaroStartup

Our team reviews every application. Shortlisted founders are notified and prepared for the pitch event.

🎤

Pitch Live to Investors

Present your startup in a structured, closed-room pitch event in front of a curated investor panel.

🚀

Raise Funding & Scale

Connect directly with interested investors, close your round, and take your startup to the next level.
